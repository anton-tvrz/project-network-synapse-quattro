`populate_sot.py` now sets `status: active` (configurable per-prefix via `pfx.get("status", ...)`) on `IpamPrefix` creation. The schema-library's `IpamPrefix` requires a `status` dropdown value (`active`, `deprecated`, `reserved`); without it, `populate_ip_prefixes` failed with `GraphQL errors: status is mandatory for IpamPrefix at status`. This mismatch was hidden until #123 unblocked the CI schema-load path and let the seed step actually run.
