CI Schema Validation and Integration Tests (Infrahub) jobs no longer hang on `POST /api/schema/load`. The CI compose stack now includes the Prefect-backed `infrahub-task-manager-db`, `infrahub-task-manager`, and `infrahub-task-worker` services (mirroring the local dev stack) that the schema-load endpoint dispatches background flows to. The workflow's readiness probe additionally waits for the task-worker to register with its work pool before proceeding. Also adds the `location_minimal` extension to the schema-load list so seed data referencing `LocationSite` resolves.
